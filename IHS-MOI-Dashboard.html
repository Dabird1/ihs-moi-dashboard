SAM vs. Penetration PositioningBubble size = Est. Revenue  ·  X = SAM  ·  Y = Penetration %
Methodology: TAM scaled from national benchmarks (IBISWorld, NAHB, Statista) by metro housing share (Atlanta ~1.75% of 137M U.S. units; Boston ~1.39%). SAM = ~70% of TAM (owner-occupied SFH within service radius). SOM = SAM × 5%. Est. Revenue = SAM × Penetration %. SOM Captured = (Penetration ÷ 5%) as a percentage — green border ≥40%, yellow 20–39%, red <20%. National View IHS Revenue counts Active markets only by default; modeled revenue from Target market sliders is added separately. Boston defaults reflect GF Sprague's ~$10M revenue profile (roofing-first). Atlanta defaults to 0% (no current operations).
